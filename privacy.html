Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Introduction

At Pahari Abirta, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. By using our site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We aim to collect minimal data. Here's what we may gather:

  • Usage Data — Anonymous, aggregated analytics such as page views and referral sources (via privacy-friendly analytics tools).
  • Local Storage — We use your browser's local storage to remember preferences like article likes and comments. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. Our website does not set any cookies on your device. We use browser local storage for a minimal set of features (like and comment state), which remains entirely on your device.

How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect is used for:

  • Understanding overall readership trends to improve our content.
  • Improving site performance and user experience.

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — For article content delivery. Firebase's privacy policy applies to their data handling.

We carefully select services that respect user privacy and do not engage in invasive tracking.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all pages.
  • Minimal data collection — we don't store what we don't need.
  • No user accounts or login systems that could be compromised.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear local storage in your browser settings to remove all locally-stored preferences.
  • Request information about any data we hold relating to you.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
